# Go-to-Market Strategy: PetFilter

## 1. Launch Phases

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Months 6-12)

**Objective**: Build awareness and demand before product is available.

#### Email List Building
- Landing page: Simple, science-forward, "Join the waitlist"
- Lead magnet: "The Science of Cat Litter Odor" (downloadable PDF)
- Target: 5,000+ email subscribers before Kickstarter launch
- Tools: Carrd or Webflow (landing page), ConvertKit (email)

#### Community Engagement
- **Reddit**: r/cats (3.7M), r/CatAdvice (400K+), r/Pets (500K+)
  - Share educational content about ammonia and litter odor
  - NOT promotional -- genuinely helpful science content
  - Build reputation as odor-science authority
- **Facebook Groups**: Cat owner groups, multi-cat household groups
- **YouTube**: Partner with cat content creators for early access/review

#### Content Marketing
- Blog posts: "Why Sprays Don't Actually Remove Cat Odor" (SEO play)
- Video: "We Measured the Ammonia in Your Litter Box" (compelling visual content)
- Infographic: Competitor comparison (sprays vs. carbon vs. water scrubbing)
- Technical whitepaper: For credibility with early adopter / engineer types

#### Influencer Seeding (5-10 creators)
- Cat YouTubers: Jackson Galaxy (5M+ subs), Helpful Vancouver Vet (600K+)
- Pet tech reviewers: Linus Tech Tips pet segment, tech lifestyle channels
- Cat Instagram accounts: @cats_of_instagram, niche cat accounts
- Offer: Free prototype unit + exclusive early access content

### Phase 2: Kickstarter Launch (Month 12-13)

**Objective**: Validate market demand and fund first production run.

#### Campaign Structure
- **Goal**: $50,000 (covers tooling + first 200 units)
- **Stretch goals**: $100K (expanded color options), $200K (mobile app MVP)
- **Duration**: 30 days

#### Reward Tiers
| Tier | Price | Includes | Limit |
|------|-------|---------|-------|
| Super Early Bird | $249 | 1× PetFilter + starter filters | 100 |
| Early Bird | $299 | 1× PetFilter + starter filters | 300 |
| Standard | $349 | 1× PetFilter + starter filters | Unlimited |
| Double Pack | $599 | 2× PetFilter + starter filters | 200 |
| Founder's Edition | $449 | 1× PetFilter + 1yr filter subscription + name engraved | 50 |

#### Campaign Marketing
- Email blast to waitlist (5K+)
- Reddit/social media announcements
- Influencer reviews go live (coordinated with launch)
- Facebook/Instagram ads targeting cat owners ($5K-10K budget)
- Product Hunt launch (day of or day after)

### Phase 3: Fulfillment + DTC Launch (Months 14-18)

**Objective**: Fulfill Kickstarter, establish DTC sales channel.

#### DTC Website (Shopify)
- Product page with video demo, sensor data visualization
- Subscription enrollment for filters
- "How It Works" page with science explanation
- Customer testimonials from Kickstarter backers

#### Amazon Launch (Month 16-18)
- Amazon FBA for logistics
- Optimized listing: A+ content, comparison charts
- Launch with 20-30 verified reviews (from Kickstarter backers)
- PPC campaign: "cat litter odor," "pet air purifier," "ammonia eliminator"
- Target: Page 1 ranking within 3 months

### Phase 4: Scale (Months 18-24)

**Objective**: Expand distribution and marketing.

- **Chewy**: Approach for online retail partnership
- **Petco/PetSmart**: Explore in-store or online partnership
- **International**: CE certification → EU launch via Amazon Europe
- **B2B**: Veterinary clinics, pet boarding facilities, shelters

## 4. Customer Retention Strategy

### 4.1 Subscription Model
- Filter subscription: $9.99/month (auto-ship every 3 months)
- Subscribers get 15% off replacement filters vs. one-time purchase
- Free shipping for subscribers

### 4.2 Post-Purchase Experience
- Day 1: Welcome email with setup guide + video
- Day 3: "How's it going?" check-in email
- Day 7: "Share your experience" prompt (review request)
- Day 30: Filter life reminder + subscription offer
- Day 60: "Air quality insights" email (if app connected)
- Day 90: First filter replacement reminder

### 4.3 Referral Program
- Give $30, Get $30: Existing customer shares link, both get $30 off
- 5% of referred sales expected via word-of-mouth
